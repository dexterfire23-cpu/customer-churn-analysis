\# 📉 Telecom Retention Campaign Targeting



\## Business Problem

A telecom company is losing customers to competitors. Rather than simply predicting churn, this project answers the real question a retention manager asks:



> "Given our at-risk customers — who should we call, who should we email, and who should we offer a deal?"

\## Key Findings

\- Contract type is the #1 churn driver — month-to-month customers churn at 42%

\- New customers (0-10 months tenure) are most at risk

\- Churned customers pay \~$15/month more than retained customers



\## Retention Campaign Segments

| Action | Customers | Risk Level |

|--------|-----------|------------|

| Call | 123 | >70% churn probability |

| Email | 259 | 40-70% churn probability |

| Offer a Deal | 1,025 | <40% churn probability |
